Correlation and Regression

Correlation and Regression

Correlation Coefficient

\[r=\frac{\sum_{i=1}^{n}(x_i-\bar{x})(y_i-\bar{y})}{\sqrt{\sum_{i=1}^{n}(x_i-\bar{x})^2\sum_{i=1}^{n}(y_i-\bar{y})^2}}\]

Pearson sample correlation coefficient.

Correlation and Regression

Linear Least-Squares Line

\[\hat{y}=a+bx,\quad b=\frac{\sum_{i=1}^{n}(x_i-\bar{x})(y_i-\bar{y})}{\sum_{i=1}^{n}(x_i-\bar{x})^2},\quad a=\bar{y}-b\bar{x}\]

Also called the regression line or line of best fit.
