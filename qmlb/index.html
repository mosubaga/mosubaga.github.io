MLB Statistics Database

MLB Stats from 1980

Explore batting records, pitching stats, and team performance from the modern era of baseball — all data starting from the 1980 season.

For more information on what the record represents, please see Metrics page.

⚠️ Data Coverage Notice: This database contains records starting from the 1980 season. Players who retired before 1980 (such as Babe Ruth or Cy Young) will not appear in search results.

What You Can Search

How to Use

  • 1 Navigate to Batting or Pitching using the top navigation.
  • 2 Enter the player's first name and last name in the search form — both fields must match.
  • 3 Click Search to retrieve the player's yearly records and career totals.
  • 4 For team stats, go to Teams, select a franchise from the dropdown, and click View Stats.
  • 5 Remember: only players and teams with records from 1980 onwards are available.
