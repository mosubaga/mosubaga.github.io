Lt. Columbo

The unassuming LAPD homicide detective who always has 'just one more thing' to ask.

The Detective

Lt. Columbo is a homicide detective with the Los Angeles Police Department. He is famous for his rumpled raincoat, his old Peugeot 403, and his deceptively humble demeanor. Columbo was special because he used his perceived 'clumsiness' to make suspects feel superior and overconfident, eventually leading them to reveal their own guilt. He was unique and charming because of his polite persistence and his constant references to his unseen wife. His habits included smoking cigars, constant talk about his 'wife', and his signature phrase, 'Just one more thing'.

The Author

Columbo was created by Richard Levinson and William Link. The character was most famously portrayed by Peter Falk in the long-running television series that began in 1968.

Characters

  • Lt. Columbo - The persistent and sharp-witted detective.
  • Mrs. Columbo - His wife (who is often mentioned but never seen).
  • Dog - Columbo's lethargic Basset Hound.
  • The Suspect - Usually a wealthy, high-status individual who thinks they've committed the perfect crime.
  • LAPD Officers - Various officers who assist Columbo on his cases.
