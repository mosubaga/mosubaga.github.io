LaTeX Syntax

What to typeRenders asWhen to use
$x^2 + y^2 = r^2$ $x^2 + y^2 = r^2$ Inline — equation flows within a sentence
$$E = mc^2$$ $$E = mc^2$$ Display — centered on its own line
