Probability is a branch in mathematics on how likely an event is to occur. Most probalilities are expressed as fractions, decimal numbers or percentages and are measured on a scaled between zero and one. If an event is impossible to occur, the probability is 0. A certain event has a probability of 1. Calculating permutations and combinations are essential on finding probabilities of an event.

To calculate permutation:

\[ _{n}P_{r} = \dfrac{n!}{(n - r)!} \]

To calculate combination:

\[ _{n}C_{r} = \dfrac{n!}{r!(n - r)!} \]
