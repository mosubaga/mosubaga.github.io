Changes

So what changed?


Change in characters

Cloud is a powerful character in this version. Unlike the previous FFT, Cloud does not start from Level 1 and is strong from the get go.

Reis can be now be a Dragoon once she joins and can equip spears. Powered up compared from the previous versions.

Beowulf is quite proficient in all magic jobs. Not that difficult to make him into am Arithmetician.


Change in gameplay

Masamune and Genji series

Yes, you can steal Masamune and Genji series in Ivalice Chronicle.
