Story

After the king of Ivalice dies, his two-year-old son, Prince Orinus, is to succeed him. However, his uncle, Duke Larg, is blocked from becoming regent, and the role goes to the king's cousin, Duke Goltanna. This division creates two factions: the White Lions, who support Larg and the "Black Lions," who support Goltanna, leading to a bloody civil war. The war is initially presented as a struggle between these two noble factions for control of the throne. The player's initial perspective is that of Ramza, who is part of Duke Larg's faction

While the dukes fight for power, a powerful church faction secretly works to manipulate the events of the war. The church's true goal is to gather the Zodiac Stones, which contain the sealed power of demons known as the "Zodiac Braves" or "Lucavi". The leader of this faction, the High Confessor, uses the war to his advantage to acquire the stones and ultimately plans to use them to open a portal to resurrect their leader, the High Seraph.

Initially a proud knight-aspirant, Ramza witnesses the brutality of the war firsthand, leading him to abandon his family's noble standing and become a mercenary. As Ramza travels through Ivalice, he becomes increasingly aware of the sinister forces at play behind the political conflict. Ramza eventually confronts the church's plot to resurrect the demons and works to prevent them from succeeding, a goal that puts him in direct opposition to his family's values and many of the powerful figures in the kingdom.

A Counterpart: Ramza's childhood friend, Delita, also becomes entangled in the war, but from a different perspective. While Ramza ends up fighting against the church, Delita uses his wits to navigate the political landscape and rise through the ranks of power. He ultimately takes a different approach than Ramza, using the war to his own ends to get revenge and bring down the powerful families who wronged him and his family. Ramza and Delita are both trying to end the war, but their methods and goals clash, leading to an ultimate confrontation between them.
