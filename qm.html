The Schrödinger Equation was published in 1926, forming the basis for Quantum Mechanics.

The initial form is:

\[ \hat{H}\Psi=E\Psi \]

\[ -\dfrac{\hbar^{2}}{2m}\bigtriangledown ^{2} \Psi +U\Psi=E\Psi = i\hbar \dfrac{\partial \Psi}{\partial t} \]


Where the solution must satisfy the condition

\[ \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} \! \Psi^*\Psi \, \mathrm{d}x = 1\]
